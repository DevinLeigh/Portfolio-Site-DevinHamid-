Spot Finder

Spot Finder is a responsive web app that provides dog owners with the ability to find highly rated dog-friendly businesses and other locations so they can plan outings and not need to leave their dogs at home.

My Process

Research

User research was conducted to determine what features would be most useful to dog owners. Three potential users were interviewed and the conclusions I drew from their feedback helped me to create user personas, illustrating who I believe to be the target audience for this app and further refining its function.

Ideation

A User Flow was created to determine what screens needed to be included for the first iteration and then exploratory sketching was done to come up with rough ideas of how each page might look.

Wireframes

Rough sketches were then made into a rapid prototype so that pain points could be identified through a round of moderated user testing.

Design

Once the desired mood of the app had been decided upon, a Style Guide was created to define the colors, typography, and styles to be used.

Mockups

After receiving critiques from fellow designers, mockups could be created for all breakpoints for XS, S, M, and L.

Conclusion

Spot Finder provides users with a single place where they can find all the information they’re looking for when searching for dog-friendly locations within a chosen area, enabling them to make the best decisions for their needs. As a responsive web app, it’s also accessible on whichever device users prefer and have access to at a given time.
