About

My Story

Hi, I’m Devin, a UI designer based in Shoreline, Washington with 15 years of experience designing forms for clinical trials and building and maintaining electronic data capture systems. My career began in theatre arts before eventually shifting my focus to work for a non-profit organization that collects data for cancer research trials. I have a passion for creating intuitive and creative designs and an expertise in organization with a keen attention to detail. Building studies and forms meant to capture specific data for stakeholders while also providing an optimal user experience has led me to specialize in UI Design.

During my certificate course with CareerFoundry I also completed a specialization in Frontend Development for Designers, which provided essential coding skills and gave me familiarity with the developer’s language, giving me a unique perspective that merges UX, UI, and frontend development. The result was this very website you’re looking at!

When I’m not working, I love spending time with my husband and daughter, watching movies, attending local sporting events and concerts, and hanging out at home with our dog and cat. I also have a small photography business where I take family photos and get to help families capture moments in time.

I’m looking for full-time remote work or hybrid work in the greater Seattle area but am open to discussing all possibilities. I’m eager to use my skills to transform ideas into user-friendly and visually appealing digital products that meet UX and business needs.
